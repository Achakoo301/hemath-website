About HemAth

From displacement to renewed purpose

In 1990, when the world turned upside down for Kashmiri Pandits, families lost far more than their homes. They lost stability, livelihoods, and a sense of belonging.

In 2018, five Kashmiri women came together with a shared vision: to empower displaced women, preserve cultural heritage, and create pathways toward economic independence.

Mission in Action

Every product tells a larger story

HemAth’s products are not separate from its mission. They reflect a commitment to preserving Kashmiri cultural heritage, supporting displaced women, and building resilient communities.

The long-term goal is a simple and accessible e-commerce experience where visitors can browse and purchase products with ease while directly supporting the mission.
