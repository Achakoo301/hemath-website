About HemAth

Empowering displaced Kashmiri women through strength, heritage, and opportunity

HemAth’s story is rooted in resilience, cultural preservation, and the belief that when women are empowered, entire communities can rise with them.

Our Story

From displacement to renewed purpose

In 1990, when the world turned upside down for Kashmiri Pandits, families lost more than just their homes. They lost a sense of belonging, livelihoods, and for many women, the ability to pursue long-held dreams.

Yet like the mighty chinar tree, the community refused to be uprooted. That spirit of resilience remains at the center of HemAth today.

In 2018, five Kashmiri women—Dr. Shakun Malik, Dr. Rajesh Mattoo, Reena Moza, Renu Tickoo Wokhlu, and Surabhi Bhan Kotru—came together with a shared vision: to recognize the untapped potential of displaced women and create pathways for dignity, independence, and hope.

Why HemAth Exists

Strength is built into our name

Named after the Kashmiri word for “strength,” HemAth is dedicated to supporting displaced Kashmiri women by intertwining the preservation of their rich cultural heritage with efforts to promote economic independence.

Our work is designed to build resilient communities, reignite dreams, and turn adversity into opportunity through meaningful programs, handmade craft, mentorship, and shared support.

Mission

What drives our work

HemAth’s mission is rooted in the belief that empowering women can transform entire communities.

We are committed to preserving and promoting Kashmiri cultural heritage, fostering economic independence through skill development, and nurturing holistic well-being and community resilience.

Vision

A future where every Kashmiri woman can thrive

We envision a world where every Kashmiri woman, irrespective of her circumstances, has the opportunity to thrive and make meaningful contributions to her community’s prosperity.

By fostering inclusivity and opportunity, we aim to create a ripple effect that transforms individual lives, uplifts families, and builds a brighter and more prosperous future for all.
