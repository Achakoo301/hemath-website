About HemAth

Strength, heritage, and opportunity

HemAth is dedicated to supporting displaced Kashmiri women by preserving cultural heritage, promoting economic independence, and building resilient communities.

Our Beginning

From loss to renewed purpose

In 1990, when the world turned upside down for Kashmiri Pandits, many families lost far more than their homes. They lost stability, livelihoods, and a deep sense of belonging.

HemAth reflects the refusal to be uprooted. It is rooted in resilience, community, and the belief that empowering women can transform families and futures.

Why HemAth Exists

Strength is built into the name

Named after the Kashmiri word for “strength,” HemAth is committed to preserving traditional skills while helping women generate sustainable income.

From handmade Aathors and embroidered textiles to tea and artisan collections, HemAth’s work connects cultural preservation with real opportunity.

Mission & Vision

What drives HemAth

Preserve Heritage

Protect and promote Kashmiri traditions through craft, design, and storytelling.

Foster Independence

Support women through skill development and meaningful economic opportunity.

Build Resilience

Strengthen families and communities by investing in women’s growth and leadership.

Looking Ahead

A mission-led e-commerce future

HemAth’s long-term goal is to create a simple, welcoming product-purchase experience where visitors can browse and buy shawls, jewelry, tea, and other products with ease.

The future shop experience should feel modern and accessible while staying rooted in HemAth’s nonprofit purpose and cultural story.
