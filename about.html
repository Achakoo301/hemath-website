About Us

Strength, heritage, and opportunity

HemAth is dedicated to supporting displaced Kashmiri women by preserving cultural heritage, promoting economic independence, and helping build resilient communities.

Our Beginning

From loss to renewed purpose

In 1990, when the world turned upside down for Kashmiri Pandits, many families lost far more than their homes. They lost stability, livelihoods, and a deep sense of belonging. For many women, dreams were interrupted by displacement and uncertainty.

Yet, like the mighty chinar tree, the community refused to be uprooted. HemAth reflects that spirit of strength, endurance, and renewal.

Founded in 2018

A vision created by five Kashmiri women

In 2018, five Kashmiri women—Dr. Shakun Malik, Dr. Rajesh Mattoo, Reena Moza, Renu Tickoo Wokhlu, and Surabhi Bhan Kotru—came together with a shared vision.

They recognized the untapped potential of displaced women and believed that by empowering them, they could uplift entire families and create lasting impact across communities.

Named after the Kashmiri word for “strength,” HemAth was founded to turn adversity into opportunity.

Our Mission

What drives HemAth

Our mission is rooted in the belief that empowering women can transform entire communities.

Preserve Cultural Heritage

We honor and promote Kashmiri traditions, craftsmanship, and identity through handmade products and community-centered storytelling.

Foster Economic Independence

We support women through skill development and meaningful opportunities that help create income, dignity, and long-term stability.

Build Resilient Communities

We nurture holistic well-being, connection, and resilience so that women, families, and communities can move toward a stronger future.

Our Vision

A future where every Kashmiri woman can thrive

HemAth envisions a world where every Kashmiri woman, regardless of her circumstances, has the opportunity to thrive and contribute meaningfully to her community’s prosperity.

By creating an environment of inclusivity and opportunity, we aim to generate a ripple effect that transforms individual lives, uplifts families, and strengthens entire communities.

EmpowerHer Champions

The backbone of HemAth’s mission

At the heart of HemAth’s work are the EmpowerHer Champions—a dedicated group of volunteers who are more than supporters. They are the driving force behind projects, initiatives, and community impact.

Their commitment helps turn HemAth’s mission into tangible outcomes, ensuring that the vision of empowering displaced women becomes a lived reality.
